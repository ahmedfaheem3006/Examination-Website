Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start an exam?

Simply create an account, choose your subject, and click "Start Exam". You'll be guided through the process step by step.

Can I pause an exam and continue later?

Your progress is saved automatically. However, the timer will continue running, so it's best to complete the exam in one sitting.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! You can take up to 3 practice exams for free. After that, you can upgrade to our premium plan for unlimited access.

How are the questions generated?

Our questions are created by expert educators and are regularly updated to match the latest exam patterns and syllabi.
